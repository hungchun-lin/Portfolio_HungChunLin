A Little About Me

Hi! I am Hung-Chun Lin, you can call me Hung-Chun or Lin.

I am a data scientist, received an M.S. in Data Science from George Washington University and an M.S. in Material Science and Engineering from National Cheng Kung University.

I have a huge interest in how data could shape our lives, thrive on digging into complex data sets and producing insightful, data-driven strategic recommendations.

I have been working as a data scientist for more than one year, during my data scientist journey, I learned a lot of concepts and developed a lot of skills, such as: data analysis, data mining, natural language processing, machine learning, text mining, data visualization, application launch version control, relational database design.

I am excited about new challenges as a data scientist/analyst.
